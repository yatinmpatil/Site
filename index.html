Retinal damage detection by Image based deep learning

Retinal diseases are known causes of visual impairment and blindness. Retinal optical coherence tomography (OCT) is an imaging technique used in the diagnosis of retinal diseases. Timely diagnosis of retinal diseases is crucial and relies on availability of expertise. Given an increase in prevalence of retinal diseases throughout the world, manual methods of diagnosis are unable to keep a pace with demand for screening services. Machine learning and Deep learning for these diagnoses are essential to solving these problems.
